RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

Based on the analysis findings, we recommend:

1. SCREENING PRIORITIZATION
   → Focus screening efforts on the 45+ age group, as they represent 
     a significant portion of cases with potentially longer recovery times.

2. CONTACT TRACING
   → Implement aggressive contact tracing for individuals with high contact 
     numbers (>10 contacts) to control spread.

3. RESOURCE ALLOCATION
   → Direct healthcare resources to the most affected regions:
     - Maharashtra (348 cases)
     - New York (347 cases)
     - Sao Paulo (346 cases)

4. TREATMENT PROTOCOLS
   → Develop age-specific treatment protocols, especially for elderly 
     patients who may require longer recovery periods.

5. EARLY WARNING SYSTEMS
   → Strengthen surveillance in regions showing rapid case growth for 
     early intervention.
