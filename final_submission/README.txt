PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This project analyzes 5,000 COVID-19 patient cases from the early outbreak phase
(January - March 2020). The analysis provides data-driven insights into:
- Patient demographics (age, gender, location)
- Infection patterns and transmission sources
- Recovery timelines and influencing factors
- Regional impact and outcomes

KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

• Total Cases Analyzed: 5,000 patients
• Recovery Rate: 68.4% (3,422 patients)

• Primary Infection Source: Contact with confirmed case (34.8%)

• Average Patient Age: 44.8 years
• Age Range: 1 - 90 years
• Mortality Rate: 11.2%
